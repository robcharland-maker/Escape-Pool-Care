A Higher Standard of Care

Reliable Service

Our reliable service ensures your pool is always balanced and safe by prioritizing the LSI from full-range water chemistry.

Our reliable service ensures your pool is always balanced and safe by prioritizing the Langelier Saturation Index (LSI) calculated from full-range water chemistry, not guesswork or single-parameter targets. By evaluating the true relationship between pH, alkalinity, calcium hardness, temperature, and total dissolved solids, we manage water in a way that protects both swimmers and equipment. This professional approach prevents scale formation, corrosion, surface damage, and premature equipment failure—issues commonly caused by oversimplified "range-only" maintenance. Unlike basic pool services that chase isolated numbers, our LSI-driven methodology delivers consistently stable water, longer-lasting pool finishes, and reduced long-term repair costs, setting a higher standard for dependable, expert pool care.

The Escape Pool Care Philosophy

Balanced Water. Protected Pools. Fewer Chemicals.

At Escape Pool Care, we believe great pool care isn't about chasing numbers — it's about maintaining balance.

Water is always trying to reach equilibrium. If it can't find balance in the water itself, it will take it from your pool's surfaces, equipment, and finishes. Our entire approach is built around preventing that from happening by managing water chemistry holistically using the LSI.

Balance Over "Range Chemistry"

Instead of keeping individual readings within generic ranges, we focus on how factors work together. When the system is balanced, water becomes stable, predictable, and non-destructive.

Calcium Is the Foundation Not the Problem

Calcium provides the stable foundation that balanced water needs to manage seasonal changes without aggressive chemical intervention.

Proactive, Not Reactive

Prevent problems instead of fixing them. Protect surfaces before damage occurs. Use only the chemicals the water actually needs.
